-- Audio Tag :
	<audio src="Path Location" controls autoplay loop muted></audio>
	Attributes :
		controls -- Make the Audio Visible (Must Need)
		loop -- The Audio Will me Play Looped
		muted -- The Audio Will Muted When Started
		autoplay -- The Audio Will Play automatically When The Page Load.

	<video src="Path Location" controls autoplay loop muted></video>
